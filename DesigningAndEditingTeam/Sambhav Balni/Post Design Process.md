Design Process
1. **Data Collection from Obsidian**  
    First, all research data related to the Uttarakhand Government schemes was collected and organized in **Obsidian**. This helped in keeping the information structured and easy to access.
    
2. **AI-Assisted Data Analysis**  
    The collected data from Obsidian was then analyzed using **AI assistance**. AI helped in understanding the information, simplifying complex details, and highlighting the most important points.
    
3. **Content Arrangement**  
    After analysis, the information was arranged into simple sections such as scheme title, beneficiaries, financial support, and required documents to ensure clarity.
    
4. **Design Ideation with AI**  
    Canva AI was used to get design and layout ideas. AI helped in suggesting suitable visuals, layouts, and color combinations for public awareness designs.
    
5. **Design Creation**  
    The final designs were created in Canva using:
    
    - Clean and simple layout
        
    - Easy-to-read Hindi text
        
    - Trustworthy color palette
        
    - Relevant images and illustrations
        
6. **Final Review**  
    The designs were reviewed and refined for better readability, spacing, and overall presentation.
