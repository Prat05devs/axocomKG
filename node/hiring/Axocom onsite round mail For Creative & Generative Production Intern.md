**Subject**: Axocom Onsite Interview | Creative & Generative Production Intern

Thank you for your interest in the Creative & Generative Production Intern position at Axocom. After reviewing your application, we are pleased to invite you to the onsite round of interviews at our office.

Below are the details of the role and the selection process for your reference.

### Role & Responsibilities

As a Creative & Generative Production Intern, you will:

- Conceptualize and execute high-quality creative assets across formats such as:
- Digital artwork and visual design
- Short films and video content
- Photo-based content and mixed media
- Written creative pieces (scripts, articles, narratives)
- Translate abstract ideas into compelling visual and narrative outputs
- Work with advanced production and generative tools to enhance creative output
- Collaborate closely with strategy, brand, and media teams to:
- Develop content for brand campaigns and media entities
- Align creative execution with campaign goals
- Iterate based on feedback and creative direction

You can read more about what we do at www.axocom.in

  

### Selection Process

Round 0 – Application & Portfolio Screening

- Evaluation based on resume and submitted portfolio/work samples.
- Deep discussion around your past work, creative process, and ownership

Round 1 – Creative & Generative Production Task

- Candidates will be given a conceptual or practical creative task to assess ideation, execution, and tool usage

Round 2 – Collaboration Round

- Assessment of your ability to work in a teams and collaboration style


Round 3 – Culture Fit & Creative Collaboration Round

- Evaluation of creative thinking, core values, and alignment with our work culture
  
### Internship Details

- Duration: 4–6 months
- Work Model: Hybrid (onsite presence required as and when needed)
- Conversion Opportunity: Outstanding performers may receive full-time employment offers

We are looking for curious, driven creators who enjoy both ideation and execution, take ownership of their work, and are excited to build meaningful creative assets.
