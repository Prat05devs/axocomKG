Thank you for your interest in the Strategy & Research Analyst Intern position. After reviewing your application, we are pleased to invite you to the onsite round of interviews at our office.

### Role & Responsibilities

As a Strategy & Research Analyst Intern, you will:

- Conduct in-depth research on the assigned topic to derive meaningful, actionable insights.
- Identify emerging trends and insights
- Develop data-driven communication and strategy frameworks
- Collaborate closely with our engineering teams to:
- Build data pipelines
- Contribute to our proprietary knowledge graph
- Translate research outputs into structured, actionable formats

1. Round 0 – Application Screening  
    Evaluation based on resume and prior work
2. Round 1 – Research & Strategy Interview (Core Evaluation)  
    Candidates will be given a topic to research and submit a structured report
3. Round 2 – Technical Collaboration Interview  
    Assessment of your ability to work with technical teams and generate data in accepted formats
4. Round 3 – Culture Fit & Work Simulation

- Duration: 4–6 months

- Conversion Opportunity: Outstanding performers may receive full-time employment offers  

We are looking for curious, driven individuals who take initiative and are comfortable working across research, strategy, and technology domains.
