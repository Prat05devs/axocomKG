**Subject**: Axocom Onsite Interview | Client Relationship & Business Development Intern

Thank you for your interest in the Client Relationship & Business Development Intern position at Axocom. After reviewing your application, we are pleased to invite you to the onsite round of interviews at our office.

Below are the details of the role and the selection process for your reference.

### Role & Responsibilities

As a Client Relationship & Business Development Intern, you will:

- Serve as the primary point of contact for assigned clients and media partners
- Understand client goals and translate them into actionable strategies
- Coordinate with internal teams to ensure smooth project execution and delivery
- Manage ongoing client relationships and ensure satisfaction and retention
- Present, pitch, and sell Axocom’s services and solutions to potential and existing clients
- Handle basic negotiations, timelines, and expectation management
- Assist in identifying new business opportunities and growth areas

You can read more about what we do at [www.axocom.in](http://www.axocom.in)

  

### Selection Process

Round 0 – Application Screening

- Evaluation based on resume and prior experience
- Review of sales, marketing, client-facing, or leadership exposure

Round 1 – Client Strategy & Sales Task

- Candidates will be given a scenario-based task to assess:
- Client understanding
- Strategic thinking
- Communication and pitching ability

Round 2 – Collaboration / Role-Play Round

- Assessment of:

	- Teamwork and coordination
	- Client handling and objection management
	- Real-world business communication skills

Round 3 – Culture Fit & Ownership Round

- Evaluation of:

	- Motivation for client-facing and sales roles
	- Accountability, decision-making, and cultural alignment

### Internship Details

- Duration: 4–6 months
- Work Model: Hybrid (onsite presence required as and when needed)
- Conversion Opportunity: Outstanding performers may receive full-time employment offers

We are looking for confident, thoughtful, and driven individuals who enjoy interacting with people, solving client problems, taking ownership of outcomes, and contributing to business growth.
