## **THE VIP MYSTERY UNRAVELS: 2025 ALLEGATIONS**

### **Key Development:**

In early 2025, **Urmila Sanawar** (actress and partner of former BJP MLA Suresh Rathore) made explosive allegations that went viral on social media.

### **Alleged VIP Identity:**

**Dushyant Kumar Gautam** (nicknamed "Gattu"), BJP National General Secretary and Rajya Sabha MP from Uttarakhand, has been named as the VIP who allegedly demanded "special services" from Ankita Bhandari.

## **EVIDENCE PRESENTED BY URMLA SANAWAR:**

### **1. Audio Recordings (Claimed):**

- Alleged conversations between resort staff discussing "VIP Gattu's demands"
    
- Purported phone calls mentioning political pressure
    
- Conversations about "arrangements for the VIP"
    

### **2. Political Connections:**

- Dushyant Gautam is a senior BJP leader from Uttarakhand
    
- Close associate of former BJP minister Vinod Arya (Pulkit's father)
    
- Regular visitor to Rishikesh area resorts
    

### **3. Timeline of VIP Visit:**

According to allegations:

- **September 19, 2022:** Dushyant Gautam was scheduled to visit Vanantara Resort
    
- Resort was preparing for his arrival
    
- Ankita was pressured on September 17-18 specifically for this visit
