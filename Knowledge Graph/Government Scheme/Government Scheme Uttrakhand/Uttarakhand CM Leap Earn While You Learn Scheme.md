## Introduction

-   The Uttarakhand government has introduce the 'CM Leap Earn While You Learn Scheme'.
-   The main objective of the CM Leap Earn While You Learn Scheme is to let students in Uttarakhand's colleges and universities earn money while they study.
-   The government wants to help students become financially independent, improve their job prospects by teaching them practical skills, and encourage them to rely on themselves.
-   Higher Education Department is the nodal department for this scheme.
-   Under the scheme, graduates, postgraduates, and research scholars will receive a remuneration of Rs. 100/- per hour.
-   The maximum monthly remuneration for students enrolled in the scheme is Rs. 6,000/-.
-   Selection under the scheme will be based on merit.
-   Preference will be given to candidates meeting the higher priority eligibility criteria.
-   Under the scheme work will be assigned in various fields in the institute.
-   It includes library operation support work, equipment operation and maintenance in laboratories and laboratory support work for practical sessions.
-   To apply for this scheme, students must be full-time regular students at their institution.
-   Detailed information about the 'CM Leap Earn While You Learn Scheme' will be available only after the release of official guidelines.

## Benefits of Scheme

-   The following benefits will be given under the "Uttarakhand CM Leap Earn While You Learn Scheme":-
    -   Graduates, postgraduates, and research students will receive a remuneration of Rs 100 per hour.
    -   The maximum monthly remuneration for students enrolled in the scheme is Rs 6,000.
    -   Remuneration will be directly deposited into their bank accounts through Electronic Clearing Service (ECS).

## Eligibility Criteria

-   Applicants must be permanent residents of Uttarakhand.
-   Students enrolled in graduate, postgraduate, and research programs are eligible to apply.
-   Students must be enrolled full-time in their respective institutions.
-   Further eligibility criteria for the 'Uttarakhand CM Leap Earn While You Learn Scheme' will be confirmed upon the release of official guidelines.
