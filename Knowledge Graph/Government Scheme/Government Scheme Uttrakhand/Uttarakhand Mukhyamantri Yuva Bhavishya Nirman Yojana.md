## Scheme Introduction: A Brief Overview

-   The Uttarakhand cabinet approved the Mukhyamantri Yuva Bhavishya Nirman Yojana on 10 December 2025.
-   The purpose of launching this scheme is to help deserving students prepare better for competitive exams so they can build strong career opportunities.
-   The scheme is expected to be carried out by the state's [Higher Education Department.](https://he.uk.gov.in/)
-   Under this initiative, young aspirants preparing for competitive examinations will be offered free online coaching.
-   A nationally recognised coaching institute will be selected to provide training to the shortlisted candidates.
-   All coaching offered through the scheme will be completely free, and selected students won't have to pay any fees.
-   Students studying in government universities, government colleges, government-aided colleges, or those currently in Class 11th or 12th can apply for online coaching based on the exam they want to prepare for.
-   Students will need to wait a little longer before they can start availing the free coaching benefits.
-   We will update the information as soon as the Uttarakhand government releases any new details about the Mukhyamantri Yuva Bhavishya Nirman Yojana.
-   Uttarakhand Government's [Devbhoomi Udyamita Yojana](https://www.govtschemes.in/uttarakhand-devbhoomi-udhyamita-yojana) is also providing the opportunity to students to receive entrepreneurship and skill training free of cost.

## Exams Covernment under the Scheme

-   The Mukhyamantri Yuva Bhavishya Nirma Yojana will cover online coaching for major competitive and entrance exams such as :-
    -   Civil Services.
    -   Railways.
    -   Defence Services.
    -   Banking exams.
    -   SSC and similar exams.
    -   GATE.
    -   CLAT.
    -   JEE.
    -   NET.
    -   NEET.
    -   And other recognized entrance tests.

## Scheme Benefits

-   Selected students will receive free online coaching under the scheme.
-   The coaching sessions will be conducted by a reputed coaching institute operating at the national level.

## Eligibility Requirement

-   Only students who hold permanent residency in Uttarakhand can apply for this scheme.
-   The applicant must be enrolled in one of the following :-
    -   A government university,
    -   A government or government-aided college,
    -   Or studying in Class 11 or Class 12.
-   The student should also be preparing for a competitive exam or an educational entrance test to qualify for the benefits.

## Required Documents

-   Domicile of Uttarakhand.
-   Mobile Number.
-   Aadhaar Card.
-   Income Certificate.
-   Photo.
-   Educational Qualification Documents.

## Steps to Apply

-   On 10 December 2025, the Uttarakhand cabinet cleared a new initiative called the Mukhyamantri Yuva Bhavishya Nirman Yojana.
-   The department in charge will now draft the official rules and publish the complete framework for how the scheme will operate.
-   After these rules are issued, the government will begin selecting well-established coaching institutes from across the country.
-   Once the institutes are shortlisted, the department will open the application window for eligible students.
-   Candidates will be able to apply using the process and platform mentioned in the official notification.
-   A fair and transparent evaluation system will be used to pick the most deserving applicants.
-   Students who make the final list will get free online coaching for major competitive exams such as UPSC, SSC, Banking, Railways, NEET, JEE, CLAT, GATE, NDA, and several others.
-   Right now, students simply need to wait for the government to release the official guidelines.
-   As soon as the detailed announcement or application link becomes available, we will share the update without delay.
