## Scheme Introduction: A Brief Overview

- Started in the year 2017, the Nanda Gaura Yojana is one of the major flagship schemes of the Uttarakhand Government.
- [Department of Women Empowerment and Child Development](https://wecd.uk.gov.in/pages/view/59-women-development) of Uttarakhand Government is the nodal department of this scheme.

- Under Nanda Gaura Yojana, the Uttarakhand Government is providing financial assistance to daughters of Uttarakhand.
- The main objective behind launching Nanda Gaura Yojana is to reduce the female infanticide, child marriage practice, gender inequality, and make them self sufficient by providing education to them.
- Nanda Gaura Yojana is also known by "Nanda Gaura Scheme".
- The assistance provided under the Nanda Gaura Yojana will be provided in 2 phases : -
    - Phase 1 benefits will be provided at the time of Birth of Girl Child, whereas
    - Phase 2 benefits will be provided when Girl passes the Class 12th.
- Financial Assistance of Rs. 11,000/- will be provided to beneficiaries in phase 1 of Nanda Gaura Yojana.
- Whereas, Financial Assistance of Rs. 51,000/- will be provided to Girl in phase 2 of Nanda Gaura Yojana of Uttarakhand Government.
- Families with an annual family income from all sources i.e. Rs 72,000/- (6,000/- per month) are eligible to receive scheme benefits.
- For this, the eligible beneficiaries need to furnish income certificates issued from Tehsil.
- Birth of a Girl Child should be in Hospital, No financial assistance will be provided if the birth of a girl child is in home.
- Financial benefits will only be applicable for 2 Girl Child per Family.
- Earlier, Offline Application Form is to be filled in order to avail the benefit of financial assistance under Nanda Gaura Scheme.
- Since the government started its official portal, the authority will not accept the offline application of Nanda Gaura Scheme.
- Beneficiaries can apply for any phase of Nanda Gaura Yojana through [Online Application Form](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/frmbirthform) which is available on [Official Portal of Nanda Gaura Yojana.](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/)
- Nanda Gaura Yojana Phase 1 Application can be submitted within six months of the birth of a girl child.
- The last date to submit Nanda Gaura Yojana Application Online for the second phase is 31 December 2024.
- Beneficiaries can submit the fresh application by December 20, 2024 and any correction in the submitted form can be done by December 31, 2024.

### Scheme Benefits

- The below mentioned financial assistance in 2 phases will be provided to all girl beneficiary under Nanda Gaura Yojana :-
    
    |Category                          |Amount      |
    |At the time of Birth          |Rs. 11,000/-|
    |After Passing Class 12th  |Rs. 51,000/-|
    |Total                                 |Rs. 62,000/-|
    

### Eligibility Requirements

- Beneficiary should be a Permanent Resident of Uttarakhand.
- Birth of a Girl Child should be Institutional. (For Phase 1)
- Girls should be Unmarried at the time of Passing Class 12th. (For Phase 2)
- The Annual Income of Family should be 6,000/- per Month i.e. 72,000/- per Year.

### Steps to Apply

- Parents can apply for financial assistance under Nanda Gaura Yojana at the time of birth of a girl child by filling [Online Application Form.](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/frmbirthform)
- [Online Application Form of Nanda Gaura Yojana](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/frmbirthform) is available at [Uttarakhand Nanda Gaura Yojana Official Portal.](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/)
- Beneficiary can fill the [Nanda Gaura Yojana Online Application Form](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/frmbirthform) in the following 2 stages :-
    - Beneficiary Details.
    - Documents Upload.
- In Stage 1 of Beneficiary Details, the below mentioned details will be filled in the registration form :-
    - Type of Residence. (Rural or Urban)
    - District, Block and Anganwadi Centre Name.
    - Name of Girl Child.
    - Mother Name.
    - Father Name.
    - Date of Birth.
    - Aadhar Number of Mother.
    - Mobile Number of Mother.
    - Place of Birth.
    - Registration Number of Birth Certificates.
    - Income Certificate Registration Number.
    - Registration Number of Domicile Certificate.
    - Annual Income of Family.
    - Caste.
    - Complete Address.
    - Bank Account Details.
- Click on Submit Button after filling all the details for stage 2.
- Now [upload all the documents](https://www.govtschemes.in/nanda-gaura-yojana#Documents%20Required%20at%20the%20Time%20of%20Birth) on the Nanda Gaura Yojana Portal.
- Preview the Application Form then Click on Submit Button to Submit it.
- Concerned officials from the department will scrutinize the application form and documents.
- Selected Beneficiary will get Phase 1 Amount of Rs. 11,000/- in their given bank account.
- Beneficiaries can also check the [Application Status of Nanda Gaura Yojana Online.](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/phaseonestatus)
- Beneficiaries must submit Nanda Gaura Online Application for Phase 1 within 6 months of child birth.

#### Steps to Apply After Passing Class 12th

- Girl Child have to apply separately for phase 2 of Nanda Gaura Yojana after passing class 12th.

- [Nanda Gaura Yojana Phase 2 Online Application Form](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/girls-interform) is available at [Nanda Gaura Yojana Official Portal.](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/)

- [Phase 2 Online Application Form of Nanda Gaura Yojana](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/girls-interform) is filled in 2 stages :-
    - Details of Beneficiary.
    - Documents Upload.
- The below mentioned details will be filled in stage 1 :-
    - Residence Type. (Urban or Rural)
    - District, Anganwadi Center and Block Name.
    - Aadhar Number of Girl.
    - Name of Girl.
    - Mother's Name.
    - Father's Name.
    - Date of Birth.
    - Caste.
    - Mother Aadhar Number.
    - Mobile Number.
    - Roll Number of Class 12th.
    - Board Name.
    - Family Annual Income.
    - Income Certificate Number.
    - Issue Date of Income Certificate.
    - Marital Status. (Should be Unmarried)
    - Permanent Address.
    - Details of Bank Account.
- Click on Submit Button for Stage 2.
- [Upload all the required documents](https://www.govtschemes.in/nanda-gaura-yojana#Documents%20Required%20at%20the%20After%20Passing%20Class%2012th) on the portal.
- Click on the Submit Button to Submit and Take a print out of the Application Form for future reference.
- Application Form of Nanda Gaura Yojana along with the documents will be verified by the concerned officials of the department.

- Selected Girls will get phase 2 amount of Rs. 51,000/- under Uttarakhand Nanda Gaura Yojana in their bank account.
- [Phase 2 Application Status of Nanda Gaura Yojana](https://nandagaura.uk.gov.in/phasetwostatus) will be checked after filling Application ID.
- Applicants must submit their Nanda Gaura Yojana Application before its deadline.
- The last date to apply for the Nanda Gaura Yojana Applications is December 31, 2024.

### Key Features of the Scheme

- Benefits of Nanda Gaura Yojana of Uttarakhand Government will only applicable for 2 girl child.
- It is mandatory to apply for financial assistance under Nanda Gaura Yojana within 6 months of girl child birth.
- Amount of phase 1 will be provided in joint bank account of mother and girl child.
- Whereas, financial assistance provided in phase 2 of Nanda Gaura Yojana will be provided in the separate bank account of girl.
- Institutional Deliveries are subject to consider eligible under Uttarakhand Nanda Gaura Yojana.
- Institutional Deliveries includes deliveries in any of the below mentioned institution : -
    - Government Hospital.
    - Private Hospital.
    - ANM Center.
- If a Girl Child is delivered in Home then she is not eligible for financial assistance.
- Birth Certificate, Domicile and Income Certificate are not needed for the beneficiaries who reside in : -
    - Balika Niketan.
    - Nari Niketan.
    - Orphanage.
