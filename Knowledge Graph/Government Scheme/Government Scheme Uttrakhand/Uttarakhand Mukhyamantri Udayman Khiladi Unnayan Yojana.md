## Scheme Introduction: A Brief Overview

-   The Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana was introduced by the Uttarakhand government in 2022.
-   The [Uttarakhand Sports Department](https://sports.uk.gov.in/) is the nodal department for this scheme.
-   The main objective of this scheme is to provide financial assistance to deserving young players who are unable to continue their studies due to financial constraints.
-   The state government provides a monthly scholarship of Rs. 1,500/- for 1 year through the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode.
-   Under this scheme, 3,900 students will be selected by the state government annually.
-   From each district, the authority will select 25 girls and 25 boys in the age group of 8-9, 9-10, 10-11, 11-12, 12-13, and 13-14.
-   To avail the benefits of the scheme, candidates must pass the selection procedure set by the concerned department.
-   State government also runs [Mukhyamantri Khiladi Protsahan Yojana](https://www.govtschemes.in/uttarakhand-mukhyamantri-khiladi-protsahan-yojana), providing beneficiaries a scholarship and assistance for sports equipment.
-   Applicants must be between 8 years to 14 years of age to apply under the Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana.
-   The selection process is conducted annually by the Uttarakhand Sports Department from May 15 to June 15.
-   Videography is conducted during the selection process to prevent any discrepancies.
-   If any candidate has objections to the selection process, they can register their objection in writing with valid evidence before the selection committee within one hour after the competition.
-   Application for the Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana will be submitted offline.
-   Authority invites its application for the year 2025 from 15 March 2025.
-   Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana application form will be accepted until 31 March 2025.

### Scheme Benefits

-   The following benefits will be provided to the eligible beneficiary under the Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana:-
    -   Scholarships will be provided to eligible young sports players.
    -   Each player will receive a monthly scholarship of Rs 1500 for one year.
    -   A total of 3,900 candidates will be selected for the scheme, consisting of 150 girls and 150 boys from each district.

### Eligibility Requirements

-   Those beneficiaries who meet all the following conditions are eligible to apply for the Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana:-
    -   Applicant must be a resident of Uttarakhand.
    -   Applicant's age must be between 8 years to 14 years.
    -   Both males and females are eligible to apply for this scheme.
    -   Applicants must be registered at the Nyaya Panchayat or ward level.
    -   Candidates who are receiving training in sports hostels or sports colleges under the Sports Authority or State Government are not eligible to apply for the scheme.
    -   Candidates are selected based on the following physical abilities:-
        -   30 Meter Flying Run.
        -   Standing Broad Jump.
        -   Forward Bend and Reach.
        -   6 x 10 Shuttle Runs.
        -   Medicine Ball Putt.
        -   600 Meter Race.

### Required Documents

-   The following documents are needed at the time of applying for the Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana:-
    -   Age certificate.
    -   Aadhar Card.
    -   Domicile Certificate.
    -   Bank Documents.
    -   Mobile No.
    -   Latest Passport Size Photograph.

### Steps to Apply

-   Under the Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana, eligible beneficiaries can apply through offline mode.
-   Applicants must submit scheme applications before its deadline.
-   The last date to submit Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana application form is March 31, 2025.
-   Visit the website of the [Uttarakhand Sports Department](https://sports.uk.gov.in/).
-   Select the '[Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Udayman Khiladi Unnayan Yojana](https://bit.ly/3wOSAsR)' from the home page to access information.
-   Download the [offline application form](https://www.govtschemes.in/sites/default/files/2024-03/%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%81%E0%A4%96%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%82%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%80%20%E0%A4%89%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%AF%E0%A5%80%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%A8%20%E0%A4%96%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%B2%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%A1%E0%A5%80%20%E0%A4%89%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A8%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%A8%20%E0%A4%AF%E0%A5%8B%E0%A4%9C%E0%A4%A8%E0%A4%BE%20%E0%A4%86%E0%A4%B5%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%A8%20%E0%A4%AA%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%A4.pdf?v=1) from the scheme information tab.
-   Take the print out of the form and fill it out accurately.
-   Attach your latest photograph to the filled-out form.
-   Include all relevant documents required with the application.
-   Submit the application form with attachments for verification by the department's constituted committee.
-   If the application is verified and deemed true, the applicant will proceed to the selection process.
-   Information about the selection procedure will be communicated to the candidate.
-   Finally, selected candidates will receive the benefits of the scheme through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode from the District Sport Offices.
