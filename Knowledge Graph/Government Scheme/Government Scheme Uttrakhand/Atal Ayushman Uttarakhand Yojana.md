## Introduction

-   After the implementation of Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY, there are some people who are unable to receive its benefits.
-   Therefore, to provide quality healthcare services to each and every citizen of the state who are in need, the Uttarakhand Chief Minister announced Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana (AAUY).
-   Under this scheme, the beneficiaries will get the benefits similar to those offered under the [PM-JAY.](https://www.govtschemes.in/ayushman-bharat-yojana)
-   Before its announcement, approximately 5 lakhs people were benefiting from PM-JAY.
-   But now after the implementation of the AAUY the number of beneficiaries has increased to 23 lakhs.
-   It means around 18 lakhs more beneficiaries will receive the benefits of free medical care under this new scheme.
-   Schemes offers the cashless treatment of five lakh in a year per family and three days pre and 15 days post hospitalization cover.
-   Treatment under the scheme will be available in government hospitals, AIIMS, and private hospitals referred by the government hospital.
-   However, in emergency cases empanelled private hospitals can also provide treatment without any referral.
-   As of now, there are 1350 types of diseases that will be treated under the scheme.
-   However, in emergency cases, empanelled private hospitals can also provide treatment without any referral.
-   To avail of the benefits of the scheme, beneficiaries need to apply for the golden card.
-   Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana application facilities are available at Common Service Centres (CSC), hospitals, and community service centers in your area.
-   For any queries or assistance about the Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana, feel free to call the toll free number 104 issued by the Uttarakhand government.
-   Moreover, you can also contact the Arogya Mitra, who will provide in-depth details about the scheme and how to receive treatment under the AAUY.
-   For the successful implementation of the scheme, the government has allocated a budget of Rs 500 cr for 2024-25.

## Benefits of Scheme

-   The Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana (AAUY) offers similar benefits as those provided by the Ayushman Bharat- Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana.
    -   Eligible beneficiaries may avail themselves of cashless treatment of Rs 5 lakh in a year.  
        
    -   This benefit is applicable to all family members irrespective of their size.  
        
    -   Additionally, three days pre and 15 days post hospitalization benefits were also provided.
    -   The scheme offers a cashless treatment, meaning beneficiaries are not required to pay any fee during their treatment.

## Eligibility Criteria

-   To receive the benefits of the Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana (AAUY), the beneficiary must fulfill the following criteria: -
    -   He/She must be a domiciled resident of Uttarakhand.
    -   Beneficiaries must possess a ration card issued under the NFSA Act.
    -   People hail from economics disadvantaged families are eligible.
    -   His/her Aadhaar must be linked with a mobile number.
    -   However, families who are registered under the CGHS or any other health schemes of the government are not eligible.

## Documents Required

-   While submitting Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana (AAUY) application form, the beneficiary needs to share their following documents: -
    -   Aadhaar Card.
    -   Address proof.
    -   Income Certificate.
    -   Caste Certificate (if required)
    -   NFSA Ration Card.
    -   MSBY Card.
    -   Bank Documents.
    -   Photograph.
    -   Mobile number.
    -   SECC household ID.  
        

## How to apply

-   Eligible applicants can apply for the Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana Golden Card offline.
-   For this, the government organizes camps in municipal offices or at the Common Service Centre.
-   Beneficiaries can also apply for the Atal Ayushman golden card by visiting the empaneled hospital.
-   Applicants need to collect the AAUY application form.
-   Fill out the details asked in the application form and
-   Affix the list of necessary documents.
-   Submit this application to the concerned hospital or at the camp.
-   Once the details and documents submitted in the application form are successfully verified.
-   The authority will issue the Atal Ayushman Uttarakhand Yojana Golden Card.
-   Note: If the beneficiary doesn't possess the golden card, they must carry their Ration Card, Aadhaar or Voter ID card in case of emergency.
