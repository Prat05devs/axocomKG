## Introduction

-   Research is very important aspect in today's human life.
-   Research plays very vital role behind every successful thing done by humans.
-   Same like that, to maintain the quality of education research is very much importantly needed.
-   Indian Government also emphasised on importance of Research in New Education Policy.
-   Now, Government of Uttarakhand steps forward to work towards research by implementing new scheme.
-   The name of scheme is "Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana".
-   It is going to be implemented in the state from the year 2023-2024.
-   The main objective behind launching Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana is to encourage researcher to do well in the field of research by providing them a monetary assistance.
-   In english the official name of scheme is "Uttarakhand Chief Minister Higher Education Research Encouragement Scheme".
-   [Department of Higher Education](https://he.uk.gov.in/) of Uttarakhand Government is the nodal department of this scheme.
-   This Scheme is also known by some other names like _"Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Scheme"_ or _"Uttarakhand Chief Minister Higher Education Research Incentive Scheme"_ or _"Uttarakhand Chief Minister Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana"_.
-   Government of Uttarakhand is going to provide One Time Grant and Monthly Financial Assistance to researchers under this scheme.
-   One Time Grant of Rs. 15,00,000/- will be provided to Teacher/ Professors or Students who are doing research in the field of betterment of education.
-   This Grant will further extended to Rs. 18,00,000/- in special circumstances.
-   Apart from that One Time Grant, A monthly financial assistance of Rs. 5,000/- will also provided to selected researcher under Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana.
-   One Time Grant under this scheme will be provided to researchers/ shodhkarta in 3 instalments.
-   Government of Uttarakhand releases the budget of Rs. 2 Crore for the year 2023-2024 for proper implementation of this scheme.
-   It is mandatory to include students in those research who proposal value is more than Rs. 5,00,000/.
-   Researchers/ Shodhkarta will apply for grant for their research by filling [Online Application Form.](https://musspyuk.samarth.ac.in/index.php/site/login-samarth)
-   [Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana Online Application Form](https://musspyuk.samarth.ac.in/index.php/site/login-samarth) is available at [Samarth Portal](https://ukadmission.samarth.ac.in/) of Uttarakhand Government.
-   More information about Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Ucch Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana can be seen in [Official Guidelines.](https://www.govtschemes.in/sites/default/files/2024-01/Uttarakhand%20Mukhyamantri%20Ucch%20Shiksha%20Shodh%20Protsahan%20Yojana%20Guidelines.pdf?v=1)

## Benefits of Scheme

-   Uttarakhand Government will provide below mentioned financial assistance to researchers/ shodhkarta for doing research under Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana :-
    -   One Time Grant of Rs. 15,00,000/- will be provided to selected researcher/ shodhkarta.
    -   In special circumstances, this One Time Grant will further extended to Rs. 18,00,000/-.
    -   Apart from One Time Grant, Monthly Financial Assistance of Rs. 5,000/- per month will also provided till the end of researcher's research.

## Eligible Areas of Research/ Shodh

-   Financial Assistance Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana will only be provided if the research/ shodh is on any of the below mentioned subject/ area :-
    
    -   Social Science.
    -   Language.
    -   Humanities.
    -   Literature.
    -   Environment.
    -   Recent Issues.
    -   Research on Development of Uttarakhand.
    -   Tourism.
    -   Traditional Science.
    -   Engineering.
    -   Areas of Broad Subject Like :-
        -   Humanities.
        -   Arts.
        -   Science.
        -   Commercial Management.
        -   Home Science.
    

## Eligibility Criteria

-   Government of Uttarakhand sets the following eligibility conditions in order to avail the benefit of Mukhyamantri Ucch Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana :-
    
    -   Researcher/ Shodhkarta should be a Resident of Uttarakhand.
    -   Following Researchers/ Shodhkarta are eligible for financial assistance :-
        -   Regular Professor and Teachers.
        -   Regular College going Students.
        -   Research Fellows.
    -   Teachers and Students doing their research in any of the below mentioned  
        college/ university are eligible to apply :-
        -   Government Colleges.
        -   Government University.
        -   Private University who received Government Grant.
        -   State University.
    

## Documents Required

-   The below mentioned documents is required at the time of applying for Uttarakhand Government's Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana :-
    -   Residence Proof of Uttarakhand.
    -   Email ID.
    -   Income Certificate.
    -   Caste Certificate. (If Applicable)
    -   Passport Size Photo.
    -   Mobile Number.
    -   Bank Account Details.
    -   Research (Shodh) Related Documents.
    -   Aadhar Card.

## How to Apply

-   Eligible Researchers can apply for grant and monthly financial assistance for research under Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana through [Online Application Form.](https://musspyuk.samarth.ac.in/index.php/site/login-samarth)
-   [Online Application Form of Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana](https://musspyuk.samarth.ac.in/index.php/site/login-samarth) is available at Samarth Portal of Uttarakhand Government.
-   Researcher will have to first [Register](https://musspyuk.samarth.ac.in/index.php/site/login-samarth) themselves on [Samarth Portal.](https://ukadmission.samarth.ac.in/)
-   Researcher will received User Name/ ID and Password on their registered mobile number.
-   [Login](https://musspyuk.samarth.ac.in/index.php/site/login-samarth) on the Samarth Portal with the help of received User ID/ Reg. Number and Password.
-   Choose Uttarkhand Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana from the scheme list.
-   Fill the below mentioned details step wise :-
    -   Personal Details.
    -   Contact Details.
    -   Education Qualification Related Details.
    -   Details of Research.
    -   Any Other Asked Information.
-   Upload all the related documents on the portal.
-   Now, Click on Submit Button to Submit the Application Form of Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana.
-   Application Form along with all the documents will be verified by the concerned officials of Department of Higher Education.
-   Researchers/ Shodhkarta from Realistic Research Category will be selected for One Time and Grant and Monthly Monetary Assistance under Mukhyamantri Uccha Shiksha Shodh Protsahan Yojana.
-   One Time Grant will be released in 3 instalments to researcher through DBT.
-   If Research Proposals of more than Rs. 5,00,000/- will be received then the researchers will be selected through Competitive Examination.
