## Introduction

-   Uttarakhand Government is committed to every section of its state.
-   And to fulfill this commitment, the government has launched numerous beneficial schemes for various categories.
-   In 2015, the government introduced a scheme aimed at upliftment of minority categories, called the Mukhyamantri Hunar Yojana.
-   As the name suggests, the objective of the scheme is to identify and hone the skills of the minority groups.
-   Under this scheme, the government will provide training to the beneficiaries in Sewing, Embroidery, Knitting, Plumbing, Electronics etc.
-   During the training period, the trainee is provided with information about the specifics of the work and related details.
-   Moreover, during training, they will also receive a stipend based on their working hours.
-   This stipend will be credited directly into their bank account.
-   The Uttarakhand Minority Welfare and Waqf Development Corporation is the nodal agency responsible for the successful implementation of the scheme in the state.
-   The benefits of the Mukhymantri Hunar Yojana extended to the people fall under the minority categories.
-   Minority categories include, Muslim, Sikhs, Christian, Buddhist, Parsi, and Jain.
-   To be eligible for the scheme, the beneficiary age must be between 18-45 years.
-   Beneficiaries may apply for the scheme offline by visiting the Minorities welfare and Waqf Department of Uttarakhand.
-   Applications that do not meet the eligibility conditions of the Mukhyamantri Hunar Yojana will be rejected by the government.
-   Therefore, it is important to read all the important guidelines before applying for the scheme.
-   After successful completion of training, the training institute also provides assistance to the trained individuals in finding the suitable employment.

## Benefits of Scheme

-   Beneficiaries of the Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Hunar Yojana will receive the following benefits: -
    -   Unemployed youth of the state will receive training in Sewing, Embroidery, Knitting, Plumbing, Electronics etc.
    -   Trainees will receive a stipend based on the number of training hours completed by them during their work.

| **Training Hours.** |  **Stipend.**   |
|-----------------|-------------|
|    100 Hrs.     | 2,000/- Rs. |
|    150 Hrs.     | 2,500/- Rs. |
|    250 Hrs.     | 3,000/- Rs. |
|    300 Hrs.     | 4,500/- Rs. |

## Eligibility Criteria

-   Only such beneficiaries will receive the benefit of Mukhyamantri Hunar Yojana, who may fulfill the following criteria as instructed for the scheme: -
    -   Applicants belong to the minority community.
    -   The age of the applicants should be between 18 to 45 years.
    -   The applicant must have passed class 5.
    -   Applicants must have completed their education either from Uttarakhand Board or Madarsa.
    -   To get training in Information and Technology course, the applicant must have passed the 10 class.
    -   The family income of rural area beneficiaries must not exceed Rs. 3,50,000/- per annum.
    -   The yearly family income of urban area beneficiaries should not be greater than Rs. 4,50,000/-.  
        

## Documents Required

-   The applicants need to submit following documents to fulfill their eligibility criteria for Mukhymantri Hunar Yojana: -
    -   Aadhaar Card.
    -   Birth Certificate.
    -   Domicile Certificate.
    -   Address Proof.
    -   Income Certificate.
    -   Academic Qualification documents.
    -   Caste Certificate.
    -   Passport size photograph.
    -   Mobile number.
    -   Documents related to bank accounts.  
        

## How to apply

-   Beneficiaries who meet the eligibility conditions may apply for the Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Hunar Yojna.
-   Applications for the Mukhymantri Hunar Yojna will be accepted through offline mode.
-   Eligible beneficiaries can obtain the scheme application form from the Uttarakhand Minorities Welfare Department office.
-   Please fill in the required details as requested in the application form and...
-   affix all the documents that fulfill your eligibility criteria.
-   Submit the duly filled application form to the department.
-   The department will verify the details and documents provided in the application.
-   Upon successful verification, the applicants will receive an SMS on their registered mobile number.
