## Introduction

-   Government of Uttarakhand is being focused on students studying in Colleges and Universities.
-   A lots of welfare scheme for students are currently running in Uttarakhand State.
-   In addition to it, on 24-08-2023, Uttarakhand Government gave the nod to start one more scheme for the welfare of students.
-   The name of Scheme will be "Uttarakhand Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana".
-   The name of scheme is made of two words which are :-
    -   "Devbhoomi" :- Means _"Devon ki Bhoomi i.e. Uttarakhand"._
    -   "Udhyamita" :- Means _"Entrepreneurship"._
-   So the main objective behind starting Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana is to make students self reliable and help them to start self employment by providing them skill and entrepreneurship training.
-   Free of Cost Skill and Entrepreneurship Training will be provided under Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana to those students who are currently studying in Colleges or Universities.
-   This helps students to start their own business or startup without relying on Government Jobs.
-   Uttarakhand Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana is also called _"Uttarakhand Devbhoomi Entrepreneurship Scheme"_ or _"Uttarakhand Devbhoomi Udhyamita Scheme"._
-   [Department of Higher Education](https://he.uk.gov.in/) of Uttarakhand Government is the nodal department of this scheme.
-   Selected students will get training regarding entrepreneurship and skill which is totally free of cost.
-   Every year 3,000 Students will be selected under this scheme.
-   To provide qualitative and better training to selected students, [Department of Higher Education](https://he.uk.gov.in/) collaborated with the [Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India](https://ediindia.ac.in/landing/) which is situated in Ahmedabad.
-   Apart from free of cost training, Boot Camp Pitching Event and Seed Funding will also organize under the Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana.
-   Principal and Teachers of Colleges and Universities will also get Entrepreneurship Mentor Training and Training of Centre of Excellence under this scheme.
-   Government also planned to add Entrepreneurship Syllabus in the upcoming Education Program.
-   For the successful implementation of the Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana, the Uttarakhand Government released a budget of Rs. 7,11,95000/- .
-   Every year, ten institutions will be selected as centres of excellence and will receive a sum of Rs 5,00,000/- as financial assistance.
-   For the year 2024-25, ten centres of excellence has been finalized for Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana.
-   The selected institution will serve as a hub in important areas such as agri-food processing, Ayush and wellness, Tourism, logistics and supply chain management, biotechnology, education tech, women entrepreneurship.
-   Under this scheme, the government aim to develop 100 startups through these center of excellence over the period of next five years.

## Benefits of Scheme

-   Government of Uttarakhand will provide the following benefits to selected students under Uttarakhand Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana :-
    -   Free of Cost Entrepreneurship and Skill Training will be provided.
    -   Entrepreneurship Training will be provided by [Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India.](https://ediindia.ac.in/landing/)
    -   3,000 Students will be Selected every year for Skill and Entrepreneurship Training.
    -   Boot Camp Pitching Event and Seed Funding will also organize for Students.

## Eligibility

-   Student should be a Resident of Uttarakhand.
-   Student should be Studying in any College or University of Uttarakhand.

## Documents Required

-   The below mentioned documents will be mandatorily needed at the time of applying for Uttarakhand Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana :-
    -   Residence Proof of Uttarakhand.
    -   Aadhar Card.
    -   Passport Size Photo.
    -   Email ID.
    -   Mobile Number.
    -   Education Related Documents.
    -   Caste Certificate. (If Applicable)
    -   Income Certificate.

## How to Apply

-   Students who are eligible and interested in the Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana may submit their applications.
-   The application form for Devbhoomi Udhyamita Yojana will be accepted online from [Department of Higher Education Website](https://duy-heduk.org/).
-   Participants need to register [online](https://duy-heduk.org/registration/participant) for Devbhoomi Udyamita Yojana.
-   Fill all the mandatory details requested in the application form and upload the required documents.
-   The nodal department will follow the procedures as earmarked in the guidelines.
-   Post verification and scrutiny applicants will be invited for their selected participation.
