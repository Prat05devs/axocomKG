## Introduction

-   Uttarakhand Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme was introduced by the Uttarakhand government in 2014.
-   The main objective of this scheme is to provide financial aid to disabled individuals engaged in the rural agriculture sector.
-   Under the scheme, beneficiary will receive monthly pension of Rs. 1,200/-
-   Person who have 20 to 40% disability can apply under this scheme.
-   Applicants must be between 18 to 60 years of age.
-   Previously, this scheme was known as the "[Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme](https://www.govtschemes.in/node/11374)".
-   Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme was implemented with modifications to its rules.
-   However, the Disabled Maintenance Pension Scheme continues to operate as before.
-   There is no income limit for eligibility under this scheme.
-   Both males and females are eligible to apply.
-   The application process for this scheme is only available offline.

## Benefits of the Scheme

-   Beneficiary will receive the following benefits under the Uttarakhand Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme:-
    -   Disabled women and men who engaged in rural agriculture sector receive a monthly pension.
    -   Every month, beneficiaries will receive Rs. 1200/-.

## Eligibility Criteria

-   Applicants must meet the following eligibility requirements to avail the benefits under the Uttarakhand Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme:-
    -   Applicant must be a resident of Uttarakhand.
    -   Age of the applicant should be in between 18 to 60 years.
    -   The applicant should not avial benefits from any other scheme by the state government.
    -   There is no income limit.

## Documents required

-   The following documents will be required at the time of applying for the 'Uttarakhand Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme':-
    -   Aadhar Card.
    -   Address proof.
    -   Bank/Post Office Account details.
    -   Mobile No.
    -   Passport size photograph (Verified by Village Head/Member Area Panchayat/District Panchayat President/Municipality Councilor/Village Development Officer).
    -   Voter Id Card.
    -   Disability Certificate. (Certified by Chief Medical Officer)

## How to Apply

-   Beneficiaries can apply offline for the 'Uttarakhand Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme'.
-   Applicants can obtain the offline application form from their nearest social welfare office.
-   Alternatively, they can download the form from the [official website](https://socialwelfare.uk.gov.in/) by following these steps:
    -   Visit the website's homepage and select the [Pension application form](https://socialwelfare.uk.gov.in/pages/display/125-application-forms-of-all-the-schemes) option.
    -   Choose "Teelu Rauteli Special Pension Scheme" from the list of schemes.
    -   After selecting the scheme, print out the form.
-   Once the form is printed, applicants should fill in all the required details accurately and affix their latest photograph in the designated section.
-   Attach all the necessary documents.
-   Finally, the completed application form should be submitted to the nearest social welfare office.
-   Upon successful verification, the department will deposit the monthly pension into the beneficiary's bank account.
