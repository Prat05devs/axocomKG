## Introduction

-   Uttarakhand Government celebrates its 23 foundation day on 9th of November 2023.
-   While addressing the people of state, Chief Minister Sh. Pushkar Singh Dhami announced to start new marriage assistance scheme in Uttarakhand.
-   The name of new marriage assistance scheme of Uttarakhand will be "Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana".
-   The main objective behind starting this scheme is to provide needed support for the marriage of a Girl.
-   This scheme is going to be known by some other name once implemented which are :- _"Uttarakhand Chief Minister Girl Marriage Assistance Scheme"_ or _"Uttarakhand Chief Minister Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana"_ or _"Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Scheme"_.
-   Government of Uttarakhand will provide the neccessary assistance needed for the marriage of a Girl under Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana.
-   Marriage Ceremony will be organised in every district to conduct the mass marriage of bride and groom.
-   All Expenses incurred on the mass marriage ceremony will be borne by the Government of Uttarakhand.
-   There might be a chance that Uttarakhand Government will also provide the financial assistance under Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana, it will only clear once the scheme is being implemented.
-   Benefits of Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana will only provided to those couples who are willing to wed in mass marriage ceremony conducted by Government of Uttarakhand.
-   Right now this is only the announcement of Government.
-   Very soon the Official Guidelines will be released.
-   Then the remaining eligibility conditions, benefits and apply process of Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana will get cleared.
-   As soon as the Guidelines will released, we will update it here.

## Benefits of Scheme

-   The below mentioned benefits will be provided to all Eligible Beneficiaries under Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana :-
    -   All Marriage Expenses will be Borne by the Government.
    -   Financial Assistance might be provided, will only clear once guidelines issued.

## Eligibility

-   According to the received information the following eligibility conditions is needed to be fulfilled to avail the benefit of Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana of Uttarakhand Government :-
    -   Bride should be a Resident of Uttarakhand.
    -   The Age of Bride should be 18 Years or More.
    -   The Age of Groom should be 21 Years or More.
    -   Remaining Eligibility Conditions will be Released Soon.

## Documents Required

-   Following Documents will expected to be needed after implementation of Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Kanya Samuhik Vivah Yojana :-
    -   Residence Proof/ Domicile of Uttarakhand.
    -   Aadhar Card of Bride.
    -   Aadhar Card of Groom.
    -   Bank Account Details.
    -   Mobile Number.
    -   Income Certificate.
