## Introduction

-   Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme is the prominent scheme of Uttarakhand Government for the welfare of disabled persons.
-   The main objective behind launching this pension scheme is to provide financial support to those disabled persons who are not able to earn money on their own.
-   [Department of Social Welfare](https://socialwelfare.uk.gov.in/) of Uttarakhand Government is the nodal department of this scheme.
-   Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme is also called "Uttarakhand Financial Assistance Scheme for Disabled Persons" or "Uttarakhand Disabled Pension Scheme".
-   Uttarakhand Government will provide monthly financial assistance as a pension to all eligible disable beneficiaries
-   Monthly Pension of Rs. 1,500/- per month will be provided to disabled beneficiary under Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme.
-   Only those Disable Beneficiary are eligible for monthly pension who belongs to BPL Category OR whose Annual Family Income should not be more than Rs. 48,000/- per Year.
-   Beneficiaries can apply for monthly disability pension through both Online and Offline Application Forms.
-   [Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme Online Application Form](https://ssp.uk.gov.in/OnlineRegistration/FrmDisabilityOnlineApplicationForm.aspx) is available on 2 portals of Government of Uttarakhand :-
    -   [Uttarakhand Samajik Suraksha Rajya Portal.](https://ssp.uk.gov.in/home.aspx)
    -   [Uttarakhand Apuni Sarkar Portal.](https://eservices.uk.gov.in/)
-   Disabled Beneficiary can apply for monthly pension on any of the above mentioned portals of Uttarakhand Government.
-   [Offline Application Form of Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme](https://www.govtschemes.in/sites/default/files/2023-02/Uttarakhand%20Disabled%20Pension%20Application%20Form.pdf?v=1) is also available at District Social Welfare Office of Uttarakhand Government.

## Benefits

-   The monthly financial assistance provided to Disable Beneficiaries under Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme are :-
    -   Pension of Rs. 1,500/- per Month.

## Eligibility

-   Applicant should be a Resident of Uttarakhand.
-   Applicant should be Disabled having Disability of 40% or More.
-   Applicant should be belong to BPL Category OR The Annual Income should be below Rs. 48,000/-.

## Document Required

-   Residence Proof/ Domicile of Uttarakhand.
-   Disability Certificate.
-   UDID Card.
-   Aadhar Card.
-   Income Certificate.
-   Passport Size Photo.
-   Copy of Family Register.
-   Mobile Number.
-   Bank Account Details.

## Online Application Process

-   Disable Beneficiary can apply for Monthly Disability Pension through [Online Application Form of Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme.](https://ssp.uk.gov.in/OnlineRegistration/FrmDisabilityOnlineApplicationForm.aspx)
-   [Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme Online Application Form](https://ssp.uk.gov.in/OnlineRegistration/FrmDisabilityOnlineApplicationForm.aspx) is available at the below mentioned portals of Government of Uttarakhand :-
    -   [Uttarakhand Samajik Suraksha Rajya Portal.](https://ssp.uk.gov.in/home.aspx)
    -   [Uttarakhand Apuni Sarkar Portal.](https://eservices.uk.gov.in/)
-   Beneficiary has to [Register](https://ssp.uk.gov.in/OnlineRegistration/FrmDisabilityOnlineApplicationForm.aspx) themselves first.
-   [Login](https://ssp.uk.gov.in/Login_Applicant/frmLoginApplicant.aspx) on the portal after registration and fill the Disability Pension Scheme Application Form.
-   Upload all the documents on the portal.
-   Click on submit button to submit Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme Application Form.
-   Application Forms of Disability Pension will be verified by District Social Welfare Officer.
-   Disability Pension of Rs. 1,500/- per Month will be transferred in the bank account of beneficiary after verification.
-   Pension Amount provided under Disability Pension Scheme will be credited once in a 4 month.
-   Disability Beneficiary can check their [Status of Application](https://ssp.uk.gov.in/PensionEntryResponse.aspx) of Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme Online.

## Offline Application Process

-   Disable Beneficiaries can also apply for Monthly Disability Pension under Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme through [Offline Application Form.](https://www.govtschemes.in/sites/default/files/2023-02/Uttarakhand%20Disabled%20Pension%20Application%20Form.pdf?v=1)
-   [Offline Application Form of Uttarakhand Disability Pension Scheme](https://www.govtschemes.in/sites/default/files/2023-02/Uttarakhand%20Disabled%20Pension%20Application%20Form.pdf?v=1) is available at Social Welfare Department District Office.
-   Collect the Application Form and fill it.
-   Attach all the required documents.
-   Submit the Application Form of Disability Pension Scheme with all the documents in Social Welfare Department District Office.
-   District Social Welfare Officer will verify the received application forms.
-   Monthly Disability Pension of Rs. 1,500/- per month will be credited in the bank account of Disable beneficiary.
