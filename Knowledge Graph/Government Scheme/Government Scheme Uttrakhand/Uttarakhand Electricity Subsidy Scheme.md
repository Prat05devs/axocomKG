## Scheme Introduction

-   On 16th of September, CM of Uttarakhand Sh. Pushkar Singh Dhami celebrated his 49th birthday.
-   He annonced lots of schemes for the welfare of people of Uttarakhand.
-   One of the major welfare scheme announced by him is "Electricity Subsidy Scheme".
-   Now Uttarakhand Government will provide subsidy on electricity bills under its new scheme.
-   [Uttarakhand Power Corporation Limited](https://www.upcl.org/) is the nodal agency of this scheme.
-   The main objective behind starting this scheme is to provide relief to middle class people from increased prices of electricity.
-   50% Subsidy on Electricity Bills will be provided to all eligible beneficiaries under Electricity Subsidy Scheme of Government of Uttarakhand.
-   Subsidy on Electricity Bills under this scheme will be provided according to the usage of consumer.
-   In Higher Himalayan Region, beneficiaries who consume up to 200 units of electricity per month are eligible to get 50% subsidy on electricity bills.
-   Whereas, beneficiaries from other region of Uttarakhand who consume 100 units of electricity montly are eligible for 50% subsidy on electricity bills under Electricity Subsidy Scheme.
-   Subsidy on Electricity Bills under Uttarakhand Electricity Subsidy Scheme is applicable for only 1 KW Domestic Electricity Connections.
-   It is to be estimated that around 11.50 lakh people will get the benefit of Electricity Subsidy Scheme of Uttarakhand.
-   Right now this much information is available for this scheme.
-   Application Form of Uttarakhand Electricity Subsidy Scheme is also not released yet.
-   Government of Uttarakhand will soon released the Official Guidelines of this scheme.
-   As soon as we get update, we will update the same.

### Scheme Benefits

-   Government of Uttarakhand will provide the following benefits to people of State under its newly announced Electricity Subsidy Scheme :-
    -   Subsidy will be provided on Monthly Electricity Consumption.
    -   50% Subsidy on Electricity Bill will be Provided.

### Eligibility Requirements

-   Subsidy of 50% on Electricity Consumption under Electricity Subsidy Scheme of Government of Uttarakhand Government will only provided to those households who fuflil the following eligibility criteria :-
    
    -   Permanent Residents of Uttarakhand are Eligible.
    -   Beneficiary should have Household Electricity Connection of 1 KW.
    -   The Monthly Electricity Consumption of Beneficiary should be :-
        -   Maximum 200 Units in Higher Himalayan Region.
        -   Maximum 100 Units in Other Regions of Uttarakhand.
    

### Required Documents

-   Documents that are required to avail the benefit of 50% subsidy on the consumption of electricity under Uttarakhand Government's Electricity Subsidy Scheme are as follows :-
    -   Aadhar Card.
    -   Recent Electricity Bill.
    -   Mobile Number.
