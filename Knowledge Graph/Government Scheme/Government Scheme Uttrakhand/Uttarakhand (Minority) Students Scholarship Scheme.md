####  Content

Uttarakhand government launched the scholarship scheme for the students studying in a recognized school who come under the minority.

- Muslim
- Christian
- Sikh
- Buddhist
- Jain
- Parsi

The scholarship will be awarded for studies in India in a government or private school from class I to class X, including such residential Government institutes and eligible private institutes selected and notified in a transparent manner by the State Government and Union Territory Administration concerned.

### **Eligibility**

- Must be a resident of Uttarakhand state
- This scholarship is payable only to Muslims, Sikhs, Christians, Buddhists, Parsis and followers of Jainism.
- The student should be studying as an institutional student in a recognized school/institute or madarsa in the state.
- Students who have secured not less than 50% marks in the previous final examination
- He/She is not receiving any other type of scholarship.
- Monthly income of parents / guardians of the student should not be more than double of BPL.
- Scholarship is permissible to all students falling under the above income limit in classes 9th and 10th.
- Students have to apply for scholarship in their school.
- There is a provision to make scholarship acceptance list available to all eligible students / students through the Block Education Officer and District Education Officer to District Social Welfare Officer.
    
- The amount of scholarship for the students of the concerned school is transferred by the District Social Welfare Officer to the joint account of the school scholarship, which is operated by the headmaster and a teacher or headmaster and head of the institution.

#### **Documents required to apply for the scholarship : -**

- Aadhar Card
- Income proof of parent / guardian (not more than six months period)
- Self Certified Minority Certificate
- Domicile Certificate
- High school marksheet
- Last year passing marksheet
- Bonafide certificate of current school
- Bank passbook of CBS account with aadhar linked

### **How to apply**

The scheme is implemented through the National Scholarship Portal (NSP). It is mandatory for all students to apply online on the website i.e. www.scholarships.gov.in.

#### Implementing Agencies:

The scheme is being implemented/funded by Ministry of Minority Affairs through the States/UT’s Administration.
