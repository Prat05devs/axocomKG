## Introduction

-   Government of Uttarakhand announced on 22 August 2023 to start Mukhyamantri Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana in the State.
-   Many Women of Uttarakhand is a active member of Women Self Help Groups and working hard to earn livelihood for their family.
-   Lots of Daily Use Items as well as Other Organic Products will manufactured by these Women Self Help Groups.
-   But most of the time they didn't get the proper market to sell their products.
-   And if they get proper market to sell their products then they didn't get the good price of their product.
-   Keeping these difficulties faced by Women Self Help Groups in mind, Government of Uttarakhand decided to start Mukhyamantri Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana.
-   This scheme is also called _"Uttarakhand Chief Minister Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana"_ or _"Uttarakhand Chief Minister Sashakt Bahna Utsav Scheme"_.
-   [Department of Women Empowerment and Child Development](https://wecd.uk.gov.in/) of Government of Uttarakhand is the nodal department of this scheme.
-   The main objective behind starting Mukhyamantri Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana is to increase the income of Women Self Help Groups by providing them a platform to sell their manufactured goods.
-   Government of Uttarakhand will organises the Exhibition on Public Places to sell the goods produced by Women Self Help Groups.
-   Women Self Help Groups will get proper price of their product in these Exhibition Stalls.
-   Government of Uttarakhand planned to organises the Exhibition in all 95 Vikaskhands to sell the final goods produced by Women Self Help Groups.
-   It is to be expected that around 2 Lakh 85 Thousand Women of 39 Thousand Women Self Help Groups will get the benefit of Mukhyamantri Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana.
-   Government of Uttarakhand sanctioned the implementation of this scheme.
-   Very Soon the Official Guidelines along with the Application Process and Application Form of Mukhyamantri Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana will be released by the Uttarakhand Government.
-   As soon as we get the update, We will update this page.

## Benefits of Scheme

-   Uttarakhand Government Organises the Exhibition on Public Places under Mukhyamantri Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana.
-   Goods Manufactured by Women Self Help Groups will be Kept on Sale in these Exhibitions.
-   These Exhibitions will Provide Exposure to Produced Goods of Women Self Help Groups with Good Price.

## Eligibility

-   The eligibility conditions required in order to avail the benefit of Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana are as follows :-
    -   Beneficiaries should be a Permanent Resident of Uttarakhand.
    -   Only Women Self Help Groups of Uttarakhand are Eligible.

## Documents Required

-   The below mentioned documents is expected to be needed at the time of applying for Uttarakhand Mukhyamantri Sashakt Bahna Utsav Yojana :-
    -   Aadhar Card of All Women Members.
    -   Mobile Number.
    -   Registration Related Documents of Women Self Help Groups.
    -   Bank Account Details.
    -   Any Document as Mentioned in Guidelines.
