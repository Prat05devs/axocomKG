# Content

Content Strategy (as per the workshop conducted by Mr. Durgesh Joshi)

>Influencer Marketing 

1. Category of the content:- Fashion, Lifestyle, Music, Art, Food, etc.

2. Size(Influencer's following):- 
    a) Nano - below 10k
    b) Micro - 10k-100k
    c) Macro - 100k-900k
    d) Mega - 1 Million+
    
3.  Type of content on various platforms:- 
    a) Instagram:- 
     i. Reel 
     ii. User Generated Content 
     iii. Story 
     iv. Link in bio
     v. Tag/Collaboration 
     
    b) YouTube:- 
     i. Dedicated Video
     ii. Integrated Video 
     iii. Shorts 
     iv. Story

	c) Facebook:- 
	   i. Groups
	   ii. Pages 
	   iii. Influencer 
	   iv. Reels 
	   v. Story 

>Strategy for Instagram Pages 

1. DOs 
   i. Consistency 
   ii. Few Hashtags(7-8) (Researched and Relevant)
   iii. Relatability 
   iv. Shareability 

2.  DON'Ts 
    i. Ads 
    ii. Follow-Unfollow 
    iii. Too many hashtags 

> Scripting Format (Instagram reels)

1. Punchline/Hook 
2. Introduction 
3. Relatability
4. Value/Appeal/Facts/Any USP
5. Shareability 
6. Call to Action
